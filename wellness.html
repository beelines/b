Fitness

During my workouts I add creatine and Trace Minerals PowerPaks to my water. The creatine supports muscle development and retention. The PowerPaks provide a clean energy boost and essential vitamins and minerals that get depleted during an intense workout. I tried pre-workout supplements with loads of caffeine back in high school and college, but found that they burn me out. My body is super sensitive to caffeine.
