Inspiration

My loved ones contribute to the majority of my inspiration. The unconditional love and support that my partner and family provide grace me with the confidence to follow my passions. Without their blessings I wouldn't be able to do what I do. Furthermore, I am grateful to all my amazing friends who continue to inspire, challenge, and reinvent me.

My wonderful home in Central Oregon, and the surrounding beautiful Pacific Northwest geography, never cease to amaze me. Mountains, rivers, lakes, desert, ocean, and old-growth forests are all within my reach.

The most inspirational TV series for me recently include: Mr. Robot and Devs. Favorite figures include Ram Dass, Trevor Hall, Alan Watts, and Aldous Huxley.
